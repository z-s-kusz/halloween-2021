The story so far...

We were all dared to stay the night of All Hallows Eve at the haunted house in our small hometown, Crystal Cove. We all know of the house as a looming figure that sits atop the highest hill at the edge of town. Rusting gates surround the old building like a maw of jagged, jutting teeth. After settling over the decades, it sits amidst weeds and neglect, broken windows smattered chaotically across its facade.

At our school, Tempest High, there are several rumors as to why the house is haunted: a witch lived there, a mother drowned her children there to punish her cheating husband, it was the site of grisly murders, or it was built on top of unmarked graves, etc.

The most prominent rumor we have heard is if you enter this house you will most likely not come out alive and, if you do, you will as Chad says, "Be totally fucked up, bro". We all took this dare and we will meet at the house's gates at 5:30PM.

Whether you took this dare out of morbid curiosity, as a way to prove what you already believe to be true, or just bragging rights, you have sealed your fate to a hellish night at Erebus Manor.
